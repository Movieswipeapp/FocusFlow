What is FocusFlow?

FocusFlow is a personal focus and productivity app for iOS. It helps you build a sustainable focus habit by adapting to you‚Äînot the other way around.

After a short onboarding, you get your Focus DNA: your optimal session length, best focus times, and how you handle distractions. The app uses this to tailor reminders, session length, and feedback to your unique profile.
